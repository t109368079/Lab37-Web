RESEARCH

In this research, Chia Yang Wu and I proposed a merge algorithm to eliminate the fixed boundaries problem in Transformer Encoding Linker Network(TELNet). Because of the limited size of the computing memory, we fixed the input window size as a small amount. However, it leads to the result of fixed boundaries in the result. In order to eliminate the boundaries of every input window, we came up with a merge algorithm using sliding-window method. We overlap the shots from the succeeding window, which we believe are the most possible shots that may link to shots in different window. We combine the key shot candidates from both window, by linking the overlapping shots to their most related key shot from both windows, we therefore eliminate the fixed boundaries of every window.
